DuaLearn Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 21, 2025

1. Information We Collect

DuaLearn collects the following information:

  • Usage Data: Progress tracking, practice statistics, and streak information stored locally on your device

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Track your memorization progress
  • Provide spaced repetition scheduling
  • Generate personalized practice recommendations
  • Sync progress across your devices (if logged in)

3. Data Storage

  • All progress data is stored locally on your device by default
  • Cloud sync is optional and uses encrypted Firebase services
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties

4. Children's Privacy

DuaLearn is suitable for all ages. For users under 13, parental consent is required for account creation.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt-out of cloud sync
  • Export your progress data

6. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, contact us at: nuurcompass@gmail.com
