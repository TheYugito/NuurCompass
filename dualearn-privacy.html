Privacy Policy for DuaLearn

Last Updated: December 8, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to DuaLearn ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use DuaLearn.

DuaLearn is designed with privacy in mind. All your personal data is stored locally on your device, and we do not collect, transmit, or share your personal information with any third parties.

2. Information We Store

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

DuaLearn stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Learning Progress: Your progress on each dua, including completed segments and quiz attempts
  • User Statistics: Your noor points, level, current streak, longest streak, and achievement data
  • Bookmarks: Duas you have bookmarked for quick access
  • Dhikr Progress: Your dhikr counter history and completed challenges
  • Sleep Mode Playlist: Your selected duas for sleep mode listening
  • App Settings: Your preferences including:
    • Language selection (your preferred language from available options)
    • Theme preferences (light/dark mode)
    • Notification settings and reminder times
    • Accessibility settings (text scaling, high contrast mode, screen reader optimization, reduce motion)
    • Daily Noor goal settings
    • Haptic feedback and sound effect preferences
  • Daily Flower Status: Record of your daily engagement with the app

Important: All of this data remains on your device. We do not have access to it, and it is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information stored locally on your device is used solely to:

  • Track your learning progress and display your achievements
  • Maintain your streak and calculate your noor level
  • Personalize your learning experience based on your preferences
  • Send you local notifications for daily reminders and streak reminders (if enabled)
  • Provide accessibility features based on your settings
  • Track your daily Noor goal progress

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All personal data is stored locally on your device using Hive, a secure local database
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not sync your personal data to any cloud servers
  • No Data Collection: We do not collect any data from your device
  • Device Security: Your data security depends on your device's security measures (passcode, biometric authentication, etc.)
  • Data Persistence: Your data remains on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data

5. Notifications

DuaLearn offers optional local notifications:

  • Daily Reminders: To help you maintain your learning habit
  • Streak Reminders: To remind you not to break your streak

These notifications are generated locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers. You can enable or disable notifications at any time in the app settings.

6. Third-Party Services

DuaLearn does not integrate with any third-party services that collect your data:

  • No Analytics: We do not use analytics or tracking services to monitor your behavior within the app
  • No Advertisements: The app does not contain any advertisements
  • No Social Media Integration: We do not connect to any social media platforms

The only external connection the app makes is when you choose to use the optional PayPal donation link, which opens in your browser and is governed by PayPal's privacy policy.

7. Children's Privacy

DuaLearn is suitable for users of all ages, including children. We take children's privacy seriously and comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and similar regulations.

  • No personal information is collected from children or any users
  • All progress and settings are stored locally on the device
  • The app does not require any registration or account creation
  • There are no in-app purchases
  • There are no advertisements
  • There is no user-generated content or social features

8. Your Data Rights

Since all personal data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: All your data is accessible through the app interface
  • Modification: You can modify your settings at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete all your data by:
    • Uninstalling the app, or
    • Clearing the app's data through your device settings

9. International Users

DuaLearn can be used worldwide. Since all personal data is stored locally on your device and we do not collect or transmit any personal information, there are no international data transfer concerns.

For users in the European Union (EU) and European Economic Area (EEA):

  • We comply with GDPR requirements
  • No personal data is processed or transferred outside your device
  • You have full control over your data as described above

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of DuaLearn after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

11. California Privacy Rights

California residents have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected (Answer: None by us)
  • Right to delete personal information (Answer: Delete app or clear app data)
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (Note: We do not sell any personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights
